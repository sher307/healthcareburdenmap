About this map

This map was built for a graduate web applications course in Spring 2026. It combines public datasets on medical debt, primary-care shortage signals, and private-equity hospital ownership to surface patterns at the state level.

The intended audience was for patients, practitioners, policymakers, or just anyone interested in the topic. Future iterations could expand on metrics of health here and offer even more insights.

The interactive version uses Leaflet for pan and zoom.
